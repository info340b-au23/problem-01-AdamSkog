Rick Sanchez

Rick Sanchez is a character in the fictionalized television show Rick and Morty. He is the grandfather of Morty, whom he goes on adventures with, and is an incredibly smart man, having created countless advanced technology, one of which is the portal gun, which he uses to travel between different locations, dimensions, and universes. He is often seen drinking alcohol and speaks frantically to others on a regular basis.
